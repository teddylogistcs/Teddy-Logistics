About the company

Trucking, without the red tape

Teddy Logistics was founded on June 30, 2026, by Esteban, Gani, and their team, with one simple vision: build a relaxed and welcoming VTC where any driver could enjoy virtual trucking without excessive requirements — just an excellent atmosphere, a friendly crew, and open roads across Europe and America.
